## Recommended Study Schedule

### Monday-Thursday
- **1 hour study session** (theory + hands-on lab)
- Focus on one session per day
- Complete the lab exercise during the session

### Friday
- **1-1.5 hour study session** (review + portfolio project work)
- Review the week's material
- Work on portfolio projects

### Weekend
- **Optional review** or additional lab practice
- Catch up on any missed sessions
- Work on portfolio projects

---

## Weekly Breakdown

### Weeks 1-2: Phishing & Unauthorized Access
- **Focus:** Email analysis, OSINT, PCAP analysis, host log analysis
- **Portfolio Projects:** Phishing Triage Playbook, Unauthorized Access IR Report
- **Key Skills:** Email header analysis, threat enrichment, network traffic analysis

### Weeks 3-4: Data Exfiltration & DDoS
- **Focus:** Data loss detection, file transfer analysis, DDoS detection and mitigation
- **Portfolio Projects:** Data Exfil Detection Blueprint, DDoS Triage Workflow
- **Key Skills:** PCAP analysis, cloud forensics, DDoS mitigation

### Weeks 5-6: Malware Analysis
- **Focus:** Static analysis, dynamic analysis, memory forensics, reverse engineering
- **Portfolio Projects:** Malware Triage Summary Report
- **Key Skills:** Malware analysis, Volatility, Ghidra

### Weeks 7-8: Detection Engineering
- **Focus:** Suricata, YARA, Sigma rule creation
- **Portfolio Projects:** Detection Rule Pack v1
- **Key Skills:** Detection rule development, rule testing

### Weeks 9-10: Live Attack & Defense
- **Focus:** Multi-stage attack simulation, incident response
- **Portfolio Projects:** Full Incident Report with Timeline, 10-Minute Incident Summary
- **Key Skills:** End-to-end incident response, rapid reporting

### Weeks 11-12: Interview Prep & Career Planning
- **Focus:** Technical interview prep, behavioral interview prep, career roadmap
- **Portfolio Projects:** Resume optimization, LinkedIn profile
- **Key Skills:** Interview skills, career planning

---

## Tips for Success

1. **Consistency is Key:** Study a little bit every day rather than cramming
2. **Hands-On Practice:** Complete all labs - they're essential for building skills
3. **Portfolio Building:** Work on portfolio projects throughout the course
4. **Take Notes:** Document key concepts and commands for quick reference
5. **Join Communities:** Engage with cybersecurity communities for support
6. **Practice Interviews:** Use the interview prep materials to practice regularly

---

## Adjusting the Schedule

If you need to adjust the schedule:

- **Faster Pace:** Complete 2 sessions per day on weekends
- **Slower Pace:** Extend to 16-18 weeks, completing 1 session every 2-3 days
- **Focus Areas:** Spend extra time on modules that align with your career goals

---

**Remember:** The goal is not just to complete the course, but to build practical skills and a strong portfolio that demonstrates your capabilities to potential employers.
